German Shepherd

German Shepherds are one of the smartest dog breeds

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Avg Weight: 50-90 lbs
  • Avg Height: 22-26 inches
  • Life Expectancy: 12-14 years

German Shepherds, are medium-sized, muscular, and agile dogs with a noble character and high intelligence. They are known for being loyal, courageous, and confident, and are often described as natural herders.
